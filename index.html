Find-A-Fire

Welcome! This website was created to ensure the safety of all during a fire crisis in your area.

Instruction: To use the website simply enter a zip code,or city name in the search bar below and you will be able to see all fires within a 2000 mile radius on Google Maps. Either click your mouse over the affected area on Google Maps or select one of the fires under “Fires in the Area” section. You can see the name of the fire by selecting the pinpoint. To reset the page you must refresh.
